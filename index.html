Research Projects

AMAR Engine - 3D Virtual World Generation Tool

AMAR Engine (AME) is a next-generation 3D tool designed to generate virtual worlds from natural language descriptions and real-world captures, combining artificial intelligence and computer graphics technology.

GitHub Clone: git@github.com:Oasis-Company/AMAR-Engine.git

Kethematics - New Field of Family Research

Kethematics is a newly proposed field of study exploring the role of family in human history and its profound impact on individuals, society, and culture, combining cognitive science and mathematical methods.

GitHub Clone: git@github.com:Oasis-Institute-of-Technology-Science/Kethematics.git

LinOasis - Linear Algebra Visualization Tool

LinOasis is an interactive web application providing visualizations and educational resources for linear algebra and matrix operations, helping students and researchers understand complex mathematical concepts.

Visit Application Access: /linoasis
